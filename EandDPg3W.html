Sort Of!


Source: https://www.instagram.com/p/CRrUZMaprNU/


In these pictures, Instagram influencer Danae Mercer shows how just changing the way she poses transforms her body--in a sense, "editing" herself in real life. "I arch my back and lift my weight OFF my legs, so I’m not actually crouching. This also prevents thigh smoosh. I have a wedgie for longer leg line[s]. I’m not breathing. And I’m using my hair to hide my neck skin folds," she explains. "I love posing. I think it’s fun and fire and here’s to body autonomy ❤️. But also just remember this stuff happens ALL THE TIME. And your unposed, super comfy self is just as INCREDIBLE AND WORTHY as anything we see online. You got this. No thigh tucks required."
