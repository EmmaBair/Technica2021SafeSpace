Safer Settings


Social media algorithms work by showing you similar content to the topics you interact with. The interwebs can leave you star-struck, but be careful! You might end up in a black hole of online negativity without even realizing it. Here are some ways to limit your social media feed and focus on the Good Stuff.



1.) Open the Instagram app, and select 'Settings'.



2.) Click on the 'Your Activity' tab.




3.) Set a reasonable time limit for yourself, after which you will be notified that you have exceeded your daily limit.
